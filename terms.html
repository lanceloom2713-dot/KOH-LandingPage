Kingdom Of Holidays Policies

Terms & Conditions

Please read all terms carefully before confirming any booking with Kingdom Of Holidays. These policies are applicable for Domestic & International travel packages, flights, hotels, transfers and activities.

Important Booking Information

Policies may vary depending on airline rules, supplier terms, festive seasons, promotional fares and availability. Customers are advised to carefully review all booking, cancellation, refund and amendment policies before making payment.

Booking Policy

Payments & Installments

Flight amount is 100% payable at booking time.
Land package booking requires ₹30,000 advance OR 50% payment, whichever is higher.
Remaining balance must be cleared 25 days before departure.
Domestic packages require full payment 15 days before travel.
International packages require full payment 21 days before travel.
Airfare is subject to change at booking time due to airline fare fluctuations.

Travel Documents

Vouchers & Confirmation

Travel vouchers are shared within 3-5 working days after final payment.
Initial itineraries are tentative and may change as per local suppliers.
Passport copies and valid ID proofs are mandatory at booking time.
PAN Card is mandatory for every booking as per RBI guidelines.
Only written communication over WhatsApp or Email will be considered valid.

Flights

Flight Booking Terms

Flights are subject to airline rules, baggage policy and fare conditions.
Airlines may cancel, reroute or reschedule flights without prior notice.
Kingdom Of Holidays only facilitates ticket booking and is not liable for airline service issues.
Customers must verify passenger details directly with airlines before departure.
Child age: 2–12 years. Infant age: below 24 months on departure date.
Covid restrictions, lockdowns or government rules may affect bookings.

Land Packages

Hotel & Tour Terms

Prices are based on twin/quad sharing basis.
Maximum 4 adults allowed in one room.
Extra guest will receive mattress or extra bed.
Personal expenses like laundry, minibar, tips & room service are excluded.
Alternate hotels of similar category may be provided if original hotel is unavailable.
Transportation will operate only as per itinerary.
Drivers may switch off AC during hill drives.
GST/TCS charges are extra as applicable.
Force majeure events may result in itinerary changes without compensation.

Activities

Water Sports Terms

Activities depend completely on weather conditions.
No refund for weather-related activity cancellations.
Kids below 8 years are not allowed for water sports.
Children 8-12 years may be denied activity based on height/weight criteria.
Water sports are strictly prohibited under influence of alcohol or drugs. Guests are advised to maintain a minimum 24-hour gap after alcohol consumption.

Need Help Regarding Policies?

For detailed clarification regarding booking, refunds, amendments or cancellation terms, please contact Kingdom Of Holidays support team before making payment.
